To enable Developer Mode on iOS 16.0, follow these steps:

1. Open the Settings app on your iOS device.
2. Tap on "General".
3. Scroll down and tap on "Device Management".
4. Tap on your developer account.
5. Tap on "Trust [developer name]".
6. Enter your device passcode if prompted.
7. Tap on "Trust" again to confirm.

Once you have completed these steps, Developer Mode will be enabled on your iOS device and you should be able to run the provisioned testing build without any issues.
